Nitro compounds

The compounds containing the functional group -NO2 are called as nitro compounds.

Examples: 

R — NO2 (Nitroalkanes)

CH3  —  NO2 (Nitromethane)

 

Nitroalkanes:

Nitroalkanes are the derivatives of hydrocarbons in which one or more hydrogen atoms of the hydrocarbons is replaced by correponding number of NO2 group.

Eg:

CH4 → CH3 — NO2

Preparation of nitroalkanes:

1. Nitration of alkane at 400oc.

Chemical properties of nitroalkanes:

Reduction of nitroalkane gives Hydroxylamine and amino.

 

Nitrobenzene :

Preparation of Nitrobenzene:

When benzene is reacted with the mixture of conc H2So4  and conc HNO3 (Nitrating mixture) at 60oc then nitrobenzene is obtained.
