To solve the complex electric circuit, kichhoff generate kichhoffs law.

There are two laws of kichhoff: 

1. Kichhoff's 1st law : Kichhoff'ss first law is also know as junction law. One or more than one electric conductor join or connected then the incomming current is equal to the outgoing current or the algebraic sum of a current at a point of junction is Zero.

2. Kichhoff's 2nd law: Kichhof's second law is also known as the loop law. Kichhoff's second law state that "The algebraic sum of emf of cell and the potential drop is equal to zero". i.e Total emf + total IR = 0

Wheatstone Bridge Principle

Wheatstone Bridge is an electric circuit that consist of four resistor in four arms three are known resistor and one is unknown. Wheat stone bridge principle is used to know the value of unknown resistor with the help of known resistors.

Wheatstone Bridge principle state that "At the balance condition the product of resistors of opposite arms of the circut is equal".

Meter bridge is made up of wooden that consist of who resistor one is known and one is unknown. And connect with the one meter long electric conductor wire. By the help of the known resistor and the one meter long wire we can find the value of unknown resistor.
