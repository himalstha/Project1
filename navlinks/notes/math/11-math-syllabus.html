The Grade 11 NEB Mathematics syllabus in Nepal covers a variety of mathematical concepts and techniques, including algebra, geometry, and trigonometry. Students study functions and their graphs, including linear, quadratic, and exponential functions. They learn about systems of linear equations, matrices, and determinants, as well as the concepts of vector algebra and three-dimensional geometry. The syllabus also covers trigonometry, including the study of angles and the relationships between the sides and angles of triangles. Students learn about inverse trigonometric functions, solutions of triangles, and the laws of sines and cosines. In addition to these core topics, the Grade 11 NEB Mathematics syllabus may also include a review of arithmetic and introductory algebra, as well as probability and statistics. The ultimate goal of the syllabus is to develop students' mathematical skills and encourage critical thinking and problem-solving.
