What are Cookies?

2.1 A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your device when you access certain websites. Cookies are used to remember your preferences and settings, to track your browsing history, and to deliver targeted advertising.

2.2 There are different types of cookies, including:

Session cookies: These cookies are temporary and expire when you close your browser.

Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device until they expire or you delete them.
