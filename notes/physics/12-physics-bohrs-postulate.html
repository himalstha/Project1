Bohr's Atomic model

Bohr's try to overcome the defect is RutherFord's atomic model in the time period of 1911. And generate Borh's two postulate and named it:

  1. 1. Bohr's I postulate
  2. 2. Bohr's II postulate

Bohr's I postulate

Bohr's I postulates states that the electron revolve around the nucleus in their permitted orbit, the angular momentum of electron is integral multiple of h/2π

i.e mvr = nh/2π

where,

m = mass of electron

v = velocity of electron

r = radius of circulat orbit

n is perpendicular quantum number

n = 1, 2, 3, 4, .....................................

Bohr's II Postulate

Bohr's second postulates states that:

1. Electron revolve around the nucleus only in permitted orbit.

2. Electron doesn't radiated energy while revolving, electron only radiated energy when it jumps from lower orbit to higher orbit.

3. Electron absorb energy when it jumps from higher orbit to lower orbit.
