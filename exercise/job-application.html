How to Write a Job application?

At first, In first line You need to write your address at the left side.

And then write the date In (March, 12 2009) Like this format.

After leaving one line write "To" and in next line write the post of receives of the company that you want to join such as (manager, assistance)

Write company name in next line and the address of the company or receiver in next line.

Then again leave one line and write the subject of the application.

So your header section is finished now.

After leaving one line write salution or greeting to the receiver (Dear Sir/ Madam)

And the write "In Response to your advertisement in (Newspaper name) date (write date) for the post of (post name) in your (company name), I wish to offer my candidate (Write your qualification) I am hardworking, honest and punctual. "

Please find enclosed my bio-data for the said purpose. If given a chance to serve you, I assure you that I shall work with almost sincerity and dedication. Hope for a favorable response.

Your truly and then Your name



Here is the format of Job application.
